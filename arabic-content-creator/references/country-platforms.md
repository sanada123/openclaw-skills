## Best Posting Times by Country

### Gulf (Saudi, UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain)
```
Weekdays:
- Morning: 7-9 AM (commute scrolling)
- Lunch: 12-2 PM
- Evening: 8-11 PM (peak — after Isha prayer)

Weekends (Friday-Saturday):
- Friday: After Jumu'ah prayer (1-3 PM), then 9-11 PM
- Saturday: 10 AM-12 PM, 8-11 PM

Ramadan shift:
- Pre-Iftar: 4-6 PM (people scrolling while waiting)
- Post-Iftar: 9 PM-2 AM (peak — people are awake late)
- Suhoor: 2-4 AM (night owls)
```

### Egypt
```
Weekdays:
- Morning: 8-10 AM
- Afternoon: 2-4 PM
- Evening: 7-10 PM (peak)

Weekends (Friday-Saturday):
- Friday: After prayer, 2-5 PM, 8-11 PM
- Saturday: 10 AM-1 PM, 7-10 PM
```

### Levant (Lebanon, Jordan, Syria, Palestine)
```
Weekdays:
- Morning: 8-10 AM
- Lunch: 1-3 PM
- Evening: 7-10 PM (peak)

Lebanon specific:
- Late night audience (11 PM-1 AM) — nightlife culture
```

### Maghreb (Morocco, Algeria, Tunisia)
```
Weekdays:
- Morning: 8-10 AM
- Afternoon: 2-4 PM (siesta culture — some break)
- Evening: 8-11 PM (peak)

Note: Same timezone as Western Europe (UTC+0/+1)
```

## Influencer Landscape by Country

### Saudi Arabia
- Mega influencers: 1M+ followers — brand deals $5K-50K+
- Categories: fashion, food, tech, comedy, Islamic content
- Key platforms: Snapchat, Instagram, X
- Rules: Saudi Content Authority requires disclosure #إعلان

### UAE  
- Most commercialized influencer market in MENA
- Luxury + lifestyle dominant
- Dubai-based influencers = regional reach
- High cost per post ($2K-20K+)

### Egypt
- Volume-based: massive audience, lower CPM
- Comedy + music dominate
- Facebook groups have massive organic reach
- Micro-influencers (10K-100K) = best ROI

### Lebanon
- Fashion + beauty powerhouse
- Instagram-first
- Celebrity culture
- French-Arabic bilingual content
